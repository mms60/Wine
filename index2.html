The Cherry Blossom Experience

Est. 2023

By Women, For Women

Sanctuary for the Sophisticated Woman

Nestled beneath cherry blossom trees, our lounge invites you to experience curated wines from renowned vineyards, paired with thoughtfully crafted charcuterie boards amidst an atmosphere of refined exclusivity. Crafted as a sanctuary by women, for women, we celebrate both quiet solitude in comfortable chairs and inspired connections around polished benches.

Every detail at Cherry Blossom Lounge combines feminine elegance with wine sophistication - from our burgundy velvet seats to floral tasting notes. Our knowledgeable sommeliers ensure each sip awakens your senses, while our seasonal menus nourish both body and spirit.

Farrah Estelle, Founder

"We create spaces where women feel empowered to savor perfection"
